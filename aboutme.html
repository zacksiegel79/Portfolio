About Me

Zack Siegel | Interactive News

I'm a newly developed interactive news journalist focused on storytelling, data visualization, and exploring how technology shapes our world.

Background

I am originally from New York, but have lived in Evanston during my four years as a Northwestern Student. Next year, I am going to begin working as an elementary school teacher in the Chicago Public School system.

Focus Areas

I like to explore how emerging technologies like AI impact education, society, and daily life.

I'm interested in questions that matter:

  • How do new tools change the way we think?
  • What's the real tradeoff when we adopt new technology into our daily lives?
  • How can we make informed decisions about the tools we use every day?
